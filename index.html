Mission Statement

At Sommet Accounting, our mission is to empower small businesses by providing exceptional financial management services. We aim to simplify financial complexities and help businesses in Jackson, Mississippi, thrive in a competitive market. Our team of experienced accountants is dedicated to offering personalized and professional services tailored to the unique needs of each client.

Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate financial experts, Sommet Accounting started with the vision of creating a reliable financial partner for local businesses. Today, we continue to grow and innovate, helping our clients achieve financial clarity and success.

Our Products and Services

  • Bookkeeping: Comprehensive bookkeeping services to keep your financial records accurate and up-to-date.
  • Payroll Management: Reliable payroll solutions to ensure timely and error-free payments to your employees.
  • Financial Transaction Assistance: Guidance in handling day-to-day financial transactions with precision and efficiency.
  • Training for Personnel: Customized training programs to enhance your team's financial management skills.
  • Tax Preparation: Expert tax preparation and filing services to minimize your liabilities and maximize your returns.
  • Financial Consulting: Strategic financial consulting to help you make informed decisions and grow your business.
